AI-DLC Lifecycle

The AI-Driven Development Life Cycle turned a product idea into requirements, design, units of work, and verified code. Each step is tagged with its level of AI autonomy, and the persona who leads it — defined by the skills and activity that level demands.

Agentic Level
L1 · AI-Assisted
Human-in-the-loop — human authors, AI suggests
PERSONA Engineer
Skills: coding · testing · debugging
L2 · AI-Augmented
Human-in-the-loop — AI drafts, human directs
PERSONA Solution Designer
Skills: requirements · domain modeling · architecture
L3 · Agentic
Human-on-the-loop — AI executes, human reviews
PERSONA Agent Orchestrator
Skills: prompting · oversight · code review
BUSINESS PARTICIPATION
Product Owner / Sponsor
Frames the problem, sets scope, value, and priorities
Engages: Requirements · Units of Work
L2 · Augmented — AI drafts, business directs
Domain Expert (SME)
Validates the real business process and rules behind the design
Engages: Workflow Planning · App Design
L1 · Assisted — human judgment, AI suggests
Approver / Reviewer
Accepts outcomes at the gates and feeds results back in
Engages: Build & Test · Operations
L1 · Assisted — human decides, AI surfaces evidence
